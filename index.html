About

At Rystad Energy, I run the Technical Project & DevOps team ('Product' in SaaS context) behind our energy data platform — owning the full lifecycle from product conception through external release, instrumented with observability, usage and performance data from day one. I've sat on the Technology department's management team since 2024 and driving the AI adoption across the org since, Dec 2025.

Before that, at Cognite, I helped customers scale with Cognite Data Fusion, an industrial digital-twin platform used across the energy sector to link sensor data, 3D models, P&IDs and ERP systems into one contextualised layer. I owned delivery, the customer–engineering relationship, and the architectural discipline — modularisation, interoperability, scale — that let one platform become the foundation for an entire ecosystem of products.

My path started differently: an MSc in Computer Science from the University of Oxford, four years building quantitative, regulatory and trade-processing systems at JPMorgan, then a series of roles that kept pulling me toward the same problem — how do you build the platform, and the team, that lets everyone else move faster and safer. That's the work I want to keep doing, as a product leader who's close enough to the engineering to earn its trust.
